Simone Colombo's website

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Cybersecurity group at King's College London working on the social foundations of cryptography with Martin R. Albrecht and Ben Dowling.
I completed my PhD at EPFL under the supervision of Bryan Ford in the DEDIS group.

My email address is . For secure communication, I’m happy to connect on Signal.
